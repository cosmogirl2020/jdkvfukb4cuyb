Welcome to Dragon Cuisene!

Here at our restaraunt you will be able to taste the delicacies of Indian and Chinese foods! Founded by Meher, Kishin, Willaim, and Liliya! If you have any questions feel free to ask any of the owners!

All of us cherish our heritage. We enjoy and appreciate our culture. What all of us love the most is the food. No doubt about it. But we see people wanting Indian and Chinese food. There are people who want the food and people who need the food. So we decided to create a food chain that is tasty and affordable. And it isnt any old restaurant. It has a mix of Indian and Chinese food. Thanks to our individual skills we were able to make this idea come to life. Now I hope you all enjoy Dragon Cuisene.
